## Summary
MISEV2023 is the ISEV consensus guideline defining minimum reporting standards for extracellular-vesicle research, updating MISEV2018 with new sections on EV release, uptake, and in vivo study. It matters because it sets the field's shared definition of adequate evidence, which any EV diagnostic or therapeutic claim is measured against. It grounds the extracellular-vesicles primitive as the standards backbone rather than a single technical result.
