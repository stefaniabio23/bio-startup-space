## Summary
Broughton et al. built DETECTR, pairing isothermal pre-amplification with a Cas12a guide RNA whose collateral cleavage activity is read out on a lateral-flow strip, and validated it on clinical SARS-CoV-2 samples. It showed CRISPR collateral cleavage could be turned into a fast, specific, point-of-care nucleic-acid test under real diagnostic pressure. This is a foundational demonstration for the crispr-diagnostics primitive.
