## Summary

Total integrated on-chip isolation of exosomes plus tumor-marker analysis with no off-chip pretreatment. Quantum dots give a more sensitive marker-abundance limit; a deep-learning model removes manual operations and returns a result within minutes. Sits at the intersection of four primitives, which is why it reads as an integration opportunity rather than a single-primitive advance.
