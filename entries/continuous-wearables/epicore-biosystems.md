## One-liner

Skin-worn microfluidic patches plus cloud analytics that read sweat biomarkers for hydration, thermal strain and emerging health signals.

## Problem

Dehydration and heat strain cause injury and performance loss in athletes and industrial workers, but hydration status is usually guessed. Sweat carries useful biomarkers that no easy wearable captured continuously.

## Workaround

Body-weight change before and after exertion, urine color charts, and subjective thirst, all crude and delayed proxies for hydration and electrolyte loss.

## Entry wedge

The Gx Sweat Patch, a consumer sweat patch built with Gatorade / PepsiCo, which put the microfluidic technology into a large branded distribution channel and validated the sensing.

## Method

Skin-worn microfluidic patches route sweat through channels with colorimetric or electrochemical reagents to quantify fluid loss and electrolytes in real time, paired with a cloud analytics platform. Built on John Rogers lab bioelectronics IP from Northwestern.

## TAM logic

Hydration and thermal-safety monitoring spans sports, industrial and occupational safety, defense and clinical research, with expansion into kidney health, women's health, malnutrition and toxin exposure. A $10B estimate reflects the addressable slice across consumer, occupational and clinical sweat-sensing.

## Validated by

$26M Series B (Feb 2025) expanded to $32M (May 2025), led by the Steele Foundation for Hope. Gx Sweat Patch consumer partnership with Gatorade / PepsiCo. Discovery Patch is an FDA Class I device. Validation and deployment with the US Department of Defense and NIH. 2024 occupational health and safety award for Connected Hydration.

## Competes with

Other biochemical-sensing wearables (Xsensio, Nutromics) and hydration-tracking wearables, though Epicore is the furthest into commercial sweat sensing.

## Complements

Sports nutrition brands (Gatorade / PepsiCo), occupational-safety programs and industrial employers, and defense and research institutions (DoD, NIH) using the platform for physiological monitoring.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** microfluidic patch manufacturing and reagent chemistry.
**Upstream of:** athletes, workers and researchers acting on hydration and thermal-strain data.

## Gaps

Most claims cover hydration and thermal strain; the expansion into kidney health, women's health and richer biomarkers is early. Total cumulative funding beyond the Series B is not cleanly disclosed; Series B valuation undisclosed.
