## One-liner

Next-generation CRO using an AI-assisted trial operating system, EHR-powered recruitment, and performance-based pricing to run faster clinical trials.

## Problem

Traditional CROs are slow, opaque, and expensive, trials routinely run over time and over budget, with 80% missing their primary endpoint timelines.

## Workaround

Traditional CROs (IQVIA, Covance, PRA), site management organisations, internal sponsor trial teams, decentralised trial platforms (Medidata, Veeva).

## Entry wedge

Performance-based pricing model, Lindus Health takes on trial risk by tying fees to milestone delivery rather than time-and-materials. This was the commercial wedge into biotech sponsors frustrated with CRO cost overruns.

## Method

AI-assisted trial operating system integrating EHR-based patient identification, protocol design support, site activation, and real-time trial monitoring, reducing the manual coordination overhead that drives CRO cost and delay.

## TAM logic

Global CRO market estimated at $92B (2025) and growing. Lindus Health positions against the inefficiency cost, trials that miss timelines cost sponsors $1M+ per day in delayed revenue. Even capturing a small share of mid-size biotech trial management represents a large market. (Source: lindushealth.com)

## Validated by

$18M Series A (2024). $55M Series B led by Balderton Capital (January 2025). Multiple completed trials across oncology and rare disease. Performance-based contracts with biotech sponsors. Named in EF (Entrepreneur First) and Y Combinator ecosystems.

## Competes with

IQVIA, Covance (Labcorp), PRA (ICON), Syneos Health, Parexel, all traditional CROs. Also Science 37, Medable (decentralised trial platforms).

## Complements

EHR vendors (Epic, Cerner) for patient identification data, EDC platforms (Medidata, Veeva Vault) for data capture, site networks for trial execution.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** Protocol finalisation, IND submission, site identification
**Upstream of:** Primary endpoint readout, regulatory submission, drug approval

## Gaps

Small team competing against CROs with thousands of employees and established pharma relationships. Performance-based model requires capital buffer to absorb trial risk. Limited track record vs. established CROs for Phase 3 pivotal trials.

## Notes

HQ: London. Founded by Meri Beckwith and Randhir Bhullar. Wes Hassan is a key contact in the EF network. The performance-based pricing model is the sharpest commercial differentiation, aligns incentives with sponsors rather than billing on hours spent.
