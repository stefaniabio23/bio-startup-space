## One-liner

Terahertz sensors and visualization chips positioned for label-free, non-destructive detection and imaging, including possible biosensing use.

## Problem

Terahertz waves can read molecular conformational and binding information that other bands miss, and they are non-ionizing and label-free. Turning that physics into compact, sensitive, affordable biosensing hardware is unsolved.

## Workaround

Optical spectroscopy, X-ray, and standard lab assays. THz sensing today is mostly laboratory time-domain spectroscopy setups, not deployable chips.
