## One-liner

Designs and manufactures nanoMIP detection reagents, synthetic antibody replacements built by proprietary solid-phase molecular imprinting, for rapid-response and high-volume diagnostics.

## Problem

Antibody supply is slow, costly, animal-dependent, and cold-chain-bound, which throttles the speed and scale of diagnostic testing, especially during outbreaks in low and middle-income countries.

## Workaround

Traditional antibody development and licensing, recombinant antibodies, and aptamers, all of which are slower to design, less stable, or more expensive to manufacture at diagnostic volumes.

## Entry wedge

Rapid-response diagnostics for outbreaks. Backed by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation to design and scale novel detection reagents in under four weeks for low-cost, high-volume tests deployable on lateral-flow devices and high-throughput platforms.

## Method

Proprietary solid-phase synthesis immobilises a molecular template, then polymerises functional monomers around it to mold a nanoscale MIP (nanoMIP) with a high-affinity binding pocket. The approach is animal-free and cell-free, produces reproducible batches, and compresses reagent design timelines to weeks. Spun out of the University of Leicester from Sergey Piletsky's molecular imprinting research.

## Validated by

Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation grant (announced February 2023) selecting the nanoMIP platform for rapid-response diagnostics; backing from Calculus Capital; tenancy in the Pioneer Group ecosystem.

## Competes with

Tozaro (MIP synthetic antibodies), Semorex, MIP Technologies / Biotage, and antibody and aptamer reagent suppliers serving diagnostics.

## Complements

Lateral-flow and rapid-test manufacturers, high-throughput diagnostic platform makers, and global health procurement programs needing low-cost stable reagents.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** template and monomer chemistry, solid-phase synthesis infrastructure
**Upstream of:** lateral-flow and point-of-care test manufacturers, high-throughput diagnostic platforms, outbreak surveillance testing

## Gaps

Independent head-to-head sensitivity and specificity data versus antibodies in deployed assays, disclosed funding totals, and evidence of reagent sales volume beyond the Gates-funded program.
