## One-liner

Photonic biosensor chips that measure biological samples at extreme sensitivity, targeting early detection of cancer and infectious disease.

## Problem

Detecting disease early requires catching very low concentrations of biomarkers in small samples. Existing lab methods are sensitive but slow, centralized, and label-dependent, limiting fast decentralized early diagnosis.

## Workaround

Central-lab immunoassays, PCR, and mass spectrometry with labels and long turnaround, poorly suited to rapid, low-cost, point-of-care early detection.

## Entry wedge

Early cancer diagnosis (with partner Qurin Diagnostics), plus Covid-19 detection and pathogen tracing in aquaculture water, using photonic chips paired with microfluidic sample handling.

## Method

A two-part biosensor: a photonic chip where sensing happens as light passes through it, and a microfluidics section where the biological assay runs in a small sample volume. Biomolecules bound to the functionalized chip surface capture targets, changing the light's properties for label-free readout. Surface functionalization chemistry is the differentiator, inherited from parent company Surfix.

## Validated by

Spin-off of Wageningen University & Research. Backed by a EUR 8.5M investment from a Dutch consortium of Qurin Diagnostics, LioniX International, PhotonDelta, and Oost NL. Works with the eastern-Netherlands universities and the Twente photonics cluster.

## Competes with

Delta Diagnostics and HyPhoX (photonic integrated biosensor chips), and other label-free early-detection platforms.

## Complements

Qurin Diagnostics (cancer assay partner), LioniX (photonic chip supply), PhotonDelta ecosystem, and clinical labs adopting decentralized early-detection tests.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** LioniX photonic chip fabrication and Surfix surface chemistry.
**Upstream of:** clinical diagnostic providers and early-detection screening programs.

## Gaps

Preclinical; no approved diagnostic yet. Funding figure reflects the EUR 8.5M consortium investment. Founding date for the diagnostics entity is approximate (parent Surfix dates to ~2011).
