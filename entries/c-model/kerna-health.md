## One-liner

AI startup building RNA foundation models to design and optimize mRNA sequences for next-generation non-vaccine mRNA therapies, founded by ex-Moderna and ex-Deep Genomics leaders.

## Problem

Most mRNA success is in vaccines; non-vaccine mRNA therapeutics need payloads that express durably and tissue-specifically, which current sequence-design tools do not reliably deliver.

## Workaround

Hand-tuned UTR and codon optimization, empirical sequence screening, and reuse of vaccine-era design rules that do not transfer well to protein-replacement therapeutics.

## Entry wedge

Payload-side RNA design. Kerna targets the sequence-design bottleneck for non-vaccine mRNA therapeutics, collaborating with partners to optimize external RNA programs while building an internal protein-replacement pipeline.

## Method

Next-generation RNA foundation models plus ML to design mRNA sequences for stability, protein output, and tissue specificity. The focus is the payload and sequence, not lipid or LNP formulation. Founders include CEO Julia Peng, Amit Deshwar (ex-SVP, Deep Genomics), and Melissa J. Moore (ex-CSO, Moderna); angels include Patrick Hsu (Arc Institute) and Jacob Kimmel (NewLimit). Emerged from stealth in January 2025 with an oversubscribed seed led by Gradient, with Humba Ventures, Tau Ventures, Pioneer Fund, and Focalpoint Partners, amount undisclosed.

## Validated by

Founder pedigree from Moderna and Deep Genomics, and a syndicate of RNA-focused investors and angels. No publications, named partnerships, or disclosed funding total yet.

## Competes with

Raina Biosciences (generative mRNA design), Atomic AI (RNA structure), InstaDeep / BioNTech, and internal mRNA design teams at Moderna and BioNTech.

## Complements

LNP delivery designers (Mana.bio, Aganitha AI), microfluidic manufacturers (Precision NanoSystems / Cytiva), and RNA-therapeutic developers needing better payloads.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** RNA sequence libraries, protein-expression readouts, foundation-model training infrastructure
**Upstream of:** LNP formulation and delivery, RNA-therapeutic clinical development
