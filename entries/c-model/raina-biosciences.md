## One-liner

MIT spin-out using its GEMORNA generative AI engine to design mRNA sequences with enhanced translational capacity, durability, and potency for therapeutic developers.

## Problem

Therapeutic mRNA must express strongly and persist long enough to matter, but sequence design is largely manual and empirical, leaving translation and stability on the table.

## Workaround

Rule-based UTR and codon optimization, empirical screening of candidate sequences, and reuse of vaccine-era mRNA designs not tuned for durable therapeutic expression.

## Entry wedge

Generative mRNA sequence design. Raina positions GEMORNA as a partner engine to optimize collaborators' mRNA therapeutics, using latent-space representations to propose sequences with experimentally validated gains, including zero-shot designs.

## Method

GEMORNA is a deep generative model that learns latent representations of mRNA and proposes sequences optimized for translational capacity and stability, paired with in-house high-throughput synthesis and experimental validation in a closed loop. Founders include CEO Jicong Cao (ex-MIT, co-founder of Bota Biosciences), CTO He Zhang (ex-Baidu Research), and chairman Timothy Lu (former MIT faculty).

## Validated by

Peer-reviewed publication in Science (August 2025): "Deep generative models design mRNA sequences with enhanced translational capacity and stability" (DOI 10.1126/science.adr8470), covered by C&EN and phys.org. Roughly $5.7M angel round at company formation. Exhibited at BIO International Convention 2025.

## Competes with

Kerna Labs (RNA foundation models), Atomic AI (RNA structure), InstaDeep / BioNTech, and internal mRNA design teams at Moderna and BioNTech.

## Complements

LNP delivery designers (Mana.bio, Aganitha AI), microfluidic manufacturers (Precision NanoSystems / Cytiva), and RNA-therapeutic developers needing higher-expressing, more durable payloads.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** mRNA sequence and translation datasets, high-throughput synthesis, generative-model infrastructure
**Upstream of:** LNP formulation and delivery, RNA-therapeutic clinical development
