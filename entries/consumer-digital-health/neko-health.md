## One-liner

Offers preventive full-body health scans combining sensors, imaging, blood tests, and physician review.

## Problem

Preventive healthcare is reactive, fragmented, and often misses early signals before disease becomes symptomatic.

## Workaround

Annual physicals, blood panels, dermatology checks, executive health screening, private GP appointments.

## Entry wedge

Stockholm clinic: Neko Health opened its first clinic in Stockholm in 2023, targeting health-conscious Swedish consumers willing to pay out of pocket for a comprehensive preventive scan. The co-founder (Daniel Ek, Spotify) provided founder brand credibility and direct consumer reach. The first product was the full-body scan appointment, sold as a one-time or subscription experience.

## Method

Multi-sensor full-body scan (skin, heart, vascular, body composition) + blood panel + physician review in a single visit.

## TAM logic

Neko positions against the global preventive health market, framing annual scans as the equivalent of a car service for the body. The broader addressable market includes employer wellness spending, concierge medicine, and the growing consumer longevity segment. The $20B+ TAM estimate reflects global private preventive health screening spend. (Source: training knowledge; no public TAM statement verified from website.)

## Validated by

$260M Series B (January 2025, led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, with General Catalyst, Lakestar, Atomico, and others) at a $1.8B valuation, bringing total funding to $325M including the $65M Series A. Operational clinics in Stockholm and London, with US expansion (NYC) planned for 2026. 100,000+ on the global waitlist; 80% of members prepay for the following year. No published clinical outcomes data on early disease detection rates at time of research.

## Competes with

Midjourney Medical (ultrasonic CT, different modality), Superpower, Function Health

## Complements

Wearable companies (Apple, Whoop, Garmin) for between-scan monitoring, GP and specialist networks for referral handoff, biomarker tracking apps (Levels, InsideTracker).

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** Consumer health interest, wearable data awareness
**Upstream of:** GP follow-up, specialist referral, ongoing monitoring

## Gaps

Neko produces a snapshot rather than continuous monitoring, with no longitudinal biological state inference between visits. The physician review component is a human bottleneck that limits throughput scaling. No published evidence base on sensitivity/specificity for early disease detection yet. Revenue model depends on repeat visits; retention economics unclear. Does not yet operate at scale outside Stockholm and London.

## Notes

Co-founded by Daniel Ek (Spotify) and Hjalmar Nilsonne. The founder brand is a significant distribution advantage in Europe. The $260M Series B (January 2025), bringing total funding to $325M at a $1.8B valuation, is a large round for a consumer health services company, suggesting investors are betting on clinic network expansion rather than near-term unit economics.
