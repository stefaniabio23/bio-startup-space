## One-liner

Clinical trial operational intelligence platform, a "control tower" for CRO and sponsor execution, delivering real-time risk alerts and trial optimization.

## Problem

Clinical trials fail or overrun on operational errors (enrollment shortfalls, site issues, data problems) that surface too late, despite massive R&D spend and fragmented, lagging data.

## Workaround

Manual CTMS tracking, spreadsheet risk logs, periodic status reports, CRO oversight teams, and siloed EDC dashboards.

## Entry wedge

Real-time integration and harmonization of all trial data into a single operational view with predictive risk alerts, wedged in through Roivant's own biotech "Vants" as first customers.

## Method

Lokavant harmonizes trial data across sources in real time and augments it with a proprietary dataset of 2,000+ trials plus third-party benchmarks to generate predictive operational insights. Its Spectrum product (launched June 2024) adds AI feasibility and enrollment forecasting with mid-study re-forecasting.

## TAM logic

Positioned against the ~$66B global clinical trial market: operational failures and overruns are a large recoverable cost pool, and predictive intervention on enrollment and risk compounds savings across a sponsor's portfolio.

## Validated by

Incubated by Roivant Health and piloted across multiple Roivant biotech Vants. $21M growth round led by Edison Partners (2022), plus an $8M Series A extension from Mitsui to expand across APAC (2023). Proprietary 2,000+ trial dataset underpins its benchmarking claims.

## Competes with

Saama, Medidata AI/Acorn, Triomics, IQVIA analytics, Veeva CDMS analytics, and internal sponsor/CRO operations dashboards.

## Complements

EDC and CTMS platforms (Medidata, Veeva) that supply the raw data, CROs running the trials, and trial design and feasibility tools upstream.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** Trial design, site and patient data generation, EDC/CTMS systems
**Upstream of:** Trial monitoring decisions, resource reallocation, timeline and go/no-go management

## Gaps

Operational intelligence, not scientific or efficacy prediction, so it does not model patient biology or outcomes. Value depends on data-integration coverage across a sponsor's stack. Funding scale is modest versus IQVIA/Medidata analytics arms. Roivant affiliation may color perceived neutrality with non-Roivant sponsors.
