## One-liner

Makes the NanoAssemblr microfluidic mixing instruments and GenVoy reagent kits that manufacture reproducible, scalable lipid nanoparticles for RNA vaccines and genetic medicines, now part of Cytiva.

## Problem

LNP quality (size, polydispersity, encapsulation) drives whether an RNA medicine works, but bulk mixing methods are irreproducible and do not transfer cleanly from bench discovery to GMP-scale production.

## Workaround

Manual bulk mixing, T-junction and pipette formulation, and bespoke scale-up processes that change particle attributes between discovery and clinical manufacture.

## Entry wedge

Reproducible microfluidic formulation. The NanoAssemblr platform gives research labs uniform LNPs on a benchtop instrument (Ignite), then scales the same NxGen mixing physics through process development (Blaze) to the automated single-use Commercial Formulation System for cGMP production.

## Method

Chip-based NxGen mixers bring an aqueous nucleic-acid stream and an ethanolic lipid stream together under controlled flow so lipids self-assemble into uniform nanoparticles, with the mixer geometry shared across scales to preserve critical quality attributes. Off-the-shelf GenVoy-ILM kits supply pre-optimized lipid mixes. Spun out of the University of British Columbia in 2010 by Pieter Cullis, Euan Ramsay, and James Taylor.

## Validated by

Widely adopted across academic and pharma RNA programs. Raised a $13.4M Series A (2015). Acquired by Danaher's Life Sciences platform (announced June 2021, price undisclosed) and fully integrated into the Cytiva brand by 2024. Received Canadian federal investment to build an RNA Medicine Biomanufacturing Centre in Vancouver.

## Competes with

Knauer (Impes), Leon Nanodrugs, Microfluidics International, Diosna / other formulation-equipment vendors, and in-house LNP process teams at large RNA developers.

## Complements

AI LNP designers (Mana.bio, Aganitha AI), mRNA payload designers (Raina, Kerna Labs), and RNA and gene-therapy developers and CDMOs needing reproducible fill of designed formulations.

## Value chain position

**Downstream of:** ionizable-lipid supply, nucleic-acid payloads, LNP formulation design
**Upstream of:** cGMP fill-finish, RNA vaccine and gene-therapy manufacturing, clinical and commercial supply

## Gaps

The Danaher acquisition price is undisclosed (the widely-cited ~$1.8B figure could not be verified). Small-mixer throughput remains a scale-up constraint. As part of Cytiva, the independent Precision NanoSystems brand is being retired.
